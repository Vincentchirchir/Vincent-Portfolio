Fieldmax – Sales & Inventory System

A custom retail platform I designed and developed to replace manual records with real‑time stock control, role‑based sales entry, accurate FIFO‑based profit, and a KPI dashboard. Built for day‑to‑day shop operations and future integrations.

Key Features

Product Upload & Stock Control

Create/update items, manage all/in‑stock counts, guard rails with FK constraints.

Sales Recording with Auto‑Fill

Type an item code to auto‑populate name/prices; validates quantity; updates stock.

FIFO Profit Engine

Batch‑aware StockEntry logic ensures profit reflects purchase history.

Role‑Based Access (RBAC)

Admins see dashboard & product tools; assistants only record sales.

Dashboard & Alerts

Revenue, profit, top items, latest sales, and low‑stock alerts at a glance.

Timezone‑Aware Reporting

Daily/weekly/monthly rollups aligned to Africa/Nairobi & week starts Sunday.
