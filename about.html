Profile

I am Vincent Chirchir, dedicated and ambitious profesional with background in Geoinformation Technology, Survey, Data Science, and Youth leadership. I have a degree in Geoinformaion Technology from Technical Univeristy of Kenya. Currently, I am a Geographic Information Systems (GIS) student at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT), focused on spatial analysis, GIS development, and building practical tools that make data useful.

Professionally, I have contributed to diverse projects where recently, during my practicum with the Municipality of Chatham-Kent’s Asset Management Department, I collected and analyzed asset data (trees, fire hydrants, traffic signals, parking assets, bus shelters & stops) and helped translate field data into decision-ready insights. I also contributed to Fibre Cable Design with SOULCO where I applied AutoCAD in planning last-mile and backbone connectvity across 47 counties in Kenya. As Data Analyst Instructor at Idata Analystics Hub I conducted educational sessions on Python for data sciece equipping learners with valuable coding skills. My practical experience also extends to Land Surveying at Ardhi House Eldoret where I gained my skills in topographic surveys, boundary surveys, and land measurements using advanced surveying equipment and technologies.My time at Ardhi House Eldoret provided me with a comprehensive understanding of the practical aspects of land surveying, as well as exposure to the importance of precise data collection and documentation. This experience has enhanced my ability to contribute effectively to surveying projects and further solidified my passion for geospatial analysis and spatial data management.

Beyond professional expertise, I am passionate about community empowerment and Youth Governance. In 2022, I was elected as a county Commisar of the Liberal Youth Movement whereI championed initiatives that engage, mentor, and mobilize youth, fostering personal growth, civic participation, and access to opportunities.
I am also founder and chairperon of Tambul Hustle Youth Group, I have played an instrumental role in building a community-driven platform that empowers youth through collective action, entrepreneurship, and financial inclusion. The group unites 21 members from our community, focusing on savings, small business support, and social development projects.

I have also developed personal project and one of them is Fieldmax Shop—a custom sales & inventory system using Flask/Django and PostgreSQL (with FIFO stock and dashboards). Fieldmax is a personal project I conceptualized, designed, and developed to solve real challenges faced in small business management. As an upcoming developer, I wanted to create a platform that automates inventory, sales logging, and profit tracking — providing small retailers like myself with tools that are typically only available to larger businesses
